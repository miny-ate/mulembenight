Good to know

FAQ

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes — just download the QR and share the image file with them. Whoever presents a valid, unscanned QR first is admitted, so treat it like cash until it's scanned.

What happens if I lose my QR code?

Use the Find My Ticket page with your phone number and M-Pesa code — your ticket status can be looked up and the QR re-downloaded if it hasn't been scanned yet.

Why does the amount I paid matter?

Each tier has a fixed price. Matching the paid amount to the tier stops mismatched or short payments from being upgraded into a higher tier by mistake.

Is my M-Pesa code shared with anyone else?

No. It's stored only to guarantee a single ticket per payment, to power the lost-ticket lookup for you specifically, and is never displayed publicly or reused.
